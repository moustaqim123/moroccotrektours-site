Essaouira Coastal & Argan Forest

Very Easy Atlantic Coast 1 Day
Book Now

Trip Overview

Escape the heat of the city for the cool breezes of the Atlantic Ocean. This day trip takes you to Essaouira, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its Portuguese fortifications, blue boats, and artistic vibe.

But before reaching the city, we explore the unique ecosystem of the Argan forest, endemic to this region. We combine a nature walk with a cultural visit, offering a perfect blend of relaxation, nature, and history.

Highlights

  • Walk along the untouched beaches of Sidi Kaouki
  • Visit a women's Argan oil cooperative
  • Spot the famous "tree-climbing goats"
  • Fresh seafood lunch by the ocean
  • Explore the ramparts and medina of Essaouira

Detailed Itinerary

Morning: Argan Forests & Beach Walk

Depart Marrakech at 08:30. On route, we stop in the Argan forest. Keep your eyes peeled for goats climbing the trees! We then head to the wild beach of Sidi Kaouki for a refreshing walk along the sand dunes where the desert meets the ocean.

Lunch: Fresh from the Sea

We head into Essaouira town for lunch. You can choose your own fresh fish at the market stalls to be grilled right in front of you, or enjoy a meal in a rooftop restaurant overlooking the sea.

Afternoon: Exploring Mogador

Spend the afternoon exploring the Skala de la Ville (ramparts), the active fishing harbor, and the medina. The atmosphere is laid-back and artistic. We return to Marrakech in the early evening.

Optional 3-Day Variant

Day 1: Marrakech → Essaouira — Argan & Medina

Morning transfer with stop at an Argan cooperative and coastal walk in Sidi Kaouki. Afternoon exploring the medina and ramparts. Overnight in Essaouira.

Day 2: Coastal Activities & Local Culture

Optional wind-sports or horse ride along the beach in the morning. Afternoon free to visit local artisan workshops and a seafood lunch. Evening at leisure in the port quarter.

Day 3: Relaxed Return via Argan Forests

Leisurely morning market visit, then return to Marrakech with scenic stops and time to enjoy coastal views before arriving late afternoon.

Optional 4-Day Coastal Explorer

Day 1: Marrakech → Essaouira — Intro

Arrival, medina visit and evening at the port.

Day 2: Argan Forest & Workshops

Visit an Argan cooperative, artisan workshops and a cooking demo with local ingredients.

Day 3: Beach Activities

Water-sports or horse riding and time to relax on Sidi Kaouki beach.

Day 4: Free Morning & Return

Final morning to explore, then return to Marrakech in the afternoon.

Accommodation & Meals

  • Accommodation: Not applicable (Day Trip).
  • Meals: Seafood Lunch typical of the region included.

What to Bring

  • Windcheater/Jacket (Essaouira is often windy)
  • Sun protection
  • Comfortable shoes for walking the medina
  • Camera

Fitness & Difficulty

Very Easy. Flat walking on sand and paved streets. Relaxed pace.

Know Before You Go

Essaouira is known as the "Windy City of Africa". It might be cooler than Marrakech, so bring an extra layer.

Best Time to Go

All Year Round. It is a perfect escape from the summer heat of Marrakech (July-August).
