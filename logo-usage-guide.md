## ‚úÖ Logo Do's

### Spacing
- Always maintain clear space around the logo
- Minimum clear space = height of one "toe" from the footprint
- Never crowd the logo with text or other elements

### Backgrounds
- **Preferred**: White or very light backgrounds
- **Acceptable**: Dark backgrounds (logo remains visible due to strong contrast)
- **Avoid**: Busy patterns, competing colors, mid-tone greens

### Sizing
- Always scale proportionally (maintain aspect ratio)
- Use vector formats (SVG) when possible for crisp rendering
- Never stretch or compress the logo

### Color Variations
- **Primary**: Full color (green + gold) - preferred
- **Monochrome**: All forest green (#043927) - acceptable
- **White**: All white - for dark backgrounds only

---

## ‚ùå Logo Don'ts

### Never:
- ‚ùå Change the proportions or aspect ratio
- ‚ùå Rotate the logo at odd angles
- ‚ùå Add effects (drop shadows, glows, bevels)
- ‚ùå Change the brand colors
- ‚ùå Separate the mountain peaks from the footprint
- ‚ùå Use low-resolution raster images when vector is available
- ‚ùå Place on backgrounds that reduce legibility
- ‚ùå Recreate or redraw the logo
- ‚ùå Alter the typography in the horizontal version
- ‚ùå Add outlines or borders
