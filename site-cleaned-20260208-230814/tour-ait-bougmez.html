The Happy Valley

Easy Aït Bougmez Valley 3 Days
Book Now

What You're Going To Do

Cultural and nature-focused trek in the Aït Bougmez valley, featuring villages, agriculture, Berber life, and easy scenic hikes. Known as the "Happy Valley" for its welcoming people and lush landscapes, this trek offers an authentic immersion into traditional Moroccan mountain culture.

Walk through apple orchards and terraced fields, visit ancient granaries with 360° views, discover dinosaur tracks dating back millions of years, and spend nights in traditional Berber gîtes where hospitality is a way of life.

Highlights

  • Apple orchards and terraced agricultural fields
  • Sidi Moussa granary with 360° panoramic views
  • Dinosaur tracks at Ibakliwin (Jurassic period)
  • Authentic nights in traditional Berber gîtes
  • Warm hospitality and cultural exchanges
  • Easy walks suitable for all fitness levels

Itinerary

Day 1: Marrakech → Aït Bougmez Valley

Depart Marrakech early morning for a scenic 4-hour drive through the Atlas Mountains. Arrive in Aït Bougmez, settle into your traditional gîte, and enjoy a welcome lunch. In the afternoon, take a gentle walk through the valley to visit local villages and apple orchards. Dinner and overnight at the gîte.

Day 2: Sidi Moussa Granary & Dinosaur Tracks

After breakfast, hike to the ancient Sidi Moussa granary perched on a hilltop, offering stunning 360° views of the valley. Continue to Ibakliwin to see the famous dinosaur footprints preserved in rock. Return to the gîte for lunch, then spend the afternoon exploring nearby villages or relaxing. Evening cultural exchange with local families.

Day 3: Valley Exploration → Return to Marrakech

Morning walk through terraced fields and traditional irrigation systems. Visit a local cooperative to learn about traditional crafts. After lunch, depart for Marrakech, arriving in the late afternoon with unforgettable memories of the Happy Valley.

Accommodation & Meals

  • Accommodation: 2 nights in traditional Berber gîtes with shared facilities. Clean, comfortable, and authentic mountain lodging.
  • Meals: Full board included (breakfast, lunch, dinner). Traditional Berber cuisine prepared with local ingredients. Vegetarian options available.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable walking shoes (hiking boots not necessary)
  • Light layers for varying temperatures
  • Sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen)
  • Camera for capturing stunning landscapes
  • Personal toiletries and medications
  • Small daypack for daily excursions

Fitness & Difficulty

This is an Easy trek suitable for all fitness levels. Daily walks are 2-4 hours at a gentle pace with minimal elevation gain. Perfect for families, seniors, and those seeking a cultural experience rather than a physical challenge.

Good to Know

  • The valley is at moderate altitude (1,800-2,200m) - no altitude sickness concerns
  • Dress modestly when visiting villages (shoulders and knees covered)
  • Basic French or Arabic phrases are appreciated by locals
  • Mobile phone signal is limited in the valley
  • Electricity may be limited in gîtes - bring a power bank

Best Time to Visit

The Happy Valley is beautiful year-round, but the best times are April to June (spring flowers and green landscapes) and September to November (apple harvest season and autumn colors). Winter (December-March) can be cold with occasional snow, while summer (July-August) is warm and pleasant.
