Rif Mountains & Chefchaouen

3 Days Moderate North Morocco

Trip Overview

Explore the lush green landscapes of Northern Morocco on this 3-day adventure. The Rif Mountains offer a completely different vibe from the Atlas, with dense forests, waterfalls, and river valleys.

The highlight of this trip is the iconic Blue City of Chefchaouen. You will combine moderate hiking in nature—visiting God's Bridge and Akchour Waterfalls—with a cultural immersion in the blue-washed streets of the Medina.

Highlights

  • Visit the stunning Akchour Waterfalls
  • Hike to "God's Bridge" natural rock formation
  • Explore Talassemtane National Park
  • Guided walking tour of Chefchaouen Medina
  • Unique blue architecture and photography

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: God's Bridge & Akchour

We start with a scenic hike in the Akchour area. We tread along the river to reach God's Bridge, a massive natural stone arch connecting two mountains. Enjoy a swim in crystal clear natural pools.

Day 2: Talassemtane National Park

Today we venture deeper into Talassemtane National Park. The trail takes us through protected fir forests and dramatic cliffs, offering fresh mountain air and serenity.

Day 3: Chefchaouen Discovery

We descend into Chefchaouen for a guided tour of the famous blue Medina. Wander through the winding azure streets, visit the Kasbah, and shop for local handicrafts before the tour concludes.

Accommodation & Meals

  • Accommodation: Hotel or Riad in Chefchaouen (typically 3-star equivalent) with charm and comfort.
  • Meals: Breakfast and Dinner included. Lunches are usually local picnics or small restaurants.

What to Bring

  • Swimwear (for Akchour pools)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Light jacket for evenings
  • Camera (Essential!)

Fitness & Difficulty

Medium. The hikes are moderate in length and difficulty. Suitable for active travelers who enjoy nature walks but don't want extreme mountaineering.

Know Before You Go

  • Chefchaouen is hilly with many stairs.
  • The Rif mountains can be cooler and greener than the south.

Best Time to Go

Spring (April-June) is magnificent for wildflowers and green forests. Autumn is also excellent.
