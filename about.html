Our Story

Driven by passion for the mountains and a commitment to authentic travel experiences.

Who We Are

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate mountaineers, Morocco Tours Gate began with a simple mission: to share the transformative power of the Atlas Mountains and Sahara Desert with the world. We started as a small team organizing local hikes and have grown into a premier adventure travel company.

We believe that adventure is for everyone. Whether you are scaling a difficult peak or enjoying a gentle walk through a flowered valley, the mountains have something to teach us all. Our goal is to facilitate these moments of discovery while respecting the environment and local communities we visit.
