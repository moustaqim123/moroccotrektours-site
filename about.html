Our Story

Driven by passion for the mountains and a commitment to authentic travel experiences.

Who We Are

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate mountaineers, Morocco Trek Tours began with a simple mission: to share the transformative power of the Atlas Mountains and Sahara Desert with the world.

We believe that adventure is for everyone. Whether you are scaling a difficult peak or enjoying a gentle walk through a flowered valley, the mountains have something to teach us all. Our goal is to facilitate these moments of discovery while respecting the environment and local communities we visit.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We are committed to eco-friendly practices, minimizing our carbon footprint, and preserving nature for future generations.

Authenticity

We work closely with local communities to provide genuine cultural experiences that benefit the local economy.

Safety First

Your safety is non-negotiable. We adhere to the highest international safety standards and protocols.

Memorable Moments

We strive to create unique, unforgettable moments that you will cherish long after you return home.
