Azaden & Tamsoult Tour - Day Options 1 to 4

Moderate Azzaden Valley 1 - 4 Days Options

Trip Overview

Explore the most popular routes through Azaden and Tamsoult villages with flexible day options. Each day includes scenic trails, traditional Berber culture, and beautiful landscapes.

Whether you want a short escape or a multi-day immersion, our Azzaden Valley treks offer a unique perspective on mountain life, far from the busier main trails.

Highlights

  • The dramatic red-soil landscape of Azzaden Valley
  • Traditional Berber villages and terraced fields
  • Tamsoult waterfalls (the highest in the region)
  • Breathtaking passes and panoramic views
  • Authentic cultural exchange with local communities
  • Expert local mountain guides

Flexible Itinerary Options

Option 1: The 1-Day Valley Escape (Express)

  • 08:30: Departure from Imlil. We begin with a steady climb towards Tizi Mzik (2,500m).
  • 11:00: Reach the pass for panoramic views of the Imlil and Azzaden Valleys.
  • 13:00: Descend into the "Red Valley" for a traditional lunch in a local Berber house in Tizi Oussem.
  • 14:30: Explore the terraced fields and ancient walnut groves of Id Issa village.
  • 17:00: Return trek to Imlil via the same pass or transport back.

Best for: Hikers with limited time who want the most scenic views of High Atlas.

Option 2: 2-Day Tamsoult Waterfalls & High Passes

  • Day 1: Trek from Imlil over Tizi Mzik pass. Afternoon hike to the Tamsoult Waterfalls (the highest in the Atlas). Overnight in the Tamsoult Refuge or a local gîte in Tizi Oussem.
  • Day 2: After breakfast, we head further down the valley to the village of Ait Aissa. Return to Imlil crossing the Tizi Oudite pass (2,200m) offering 360-degree views.

Best for: Nature lovers who want to see the spectacular cascades and experience a night in a mountain village.

Option 3: 3-Day Villages & Cultural Immersion

  • Day 1: Start from Imlil, trek along the valley floor to the village of Aguersioual, then climb towards Tizi Oudite. Overnight in Matat village.
  • Day 2: Deep trek into the Azzaden Valley. Visit multiple Berber villages (Id Issa, Tizi Oussem). Learn about local agriculture and stay with a local family.
  • Day 3: Morning visit to Tamsoult waterfalls followed by a climb to Tizi Mzik pass for a grand finale descent back to Imlil.

Best for: Those seeking a slow-paced, authentic cultural connection with the local Berber people.

Option 4: 4-Day Grand Azzaden Discovery Loop

  • Day 1: Trek from Imlil to the remote village of Ghoummat. Experience the untouched side of the Atlas.
  • Day 2: Cross the high ridges to enter Azzaden Valley from the north. Descend into the lush groves of Ait Aissa.
  • Day 3: Full day exploring the Tamsoult region, including the waterfalls and the higher sheep folds (Azibs) where nomads stay.
  • Day 4: Sunrise hike to a viewpoint overlooking the Ait Mizane Valley, followed by a descent into Imlil for lunch and departure.

Included: Full porter/mule support, all meals prepared by our cook, and stays in the most authentic family guest houses.

Best for: Trekking enthusiasts looking for a complete, off-the-beaten-path mountain adventure.

Accommodation & Meals

  • Accommodation: Local Berber gîtes (guest houses) or Tamsoult Refuge.
  • Meals: Full board (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner) included during the trek.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable hiking boots
  • Small backpack for day use
  • Waterproof jacket
  • Personal medications
  • Camera and sunscreen
