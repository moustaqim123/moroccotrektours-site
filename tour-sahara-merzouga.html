3-Day Merzouga Desert Discovery

Easy / Moderate Marrakech to Merzouga 3 Days
Book Now

Trip Overview

The 3-day desert tour from Marrakech to Merzouga is the most popular and iconic journey in Morocco. It offers a perfect blend of dramatic landscapes, ancient history, and the magical experience of the Sahara Desert.

You'll cross the majestic High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass, explore the UNESCO World Heritage site of Ait Ben Haddou, and wander through the stunning Todra Gorges. The highlight of the trip is a camel trek into the golden dunes of Erg Chebbi, where you'll spend an unforgettable night in a traditional Berber camp under a canopy of stars.

Key Highlights

  • Crossing the High Atlas Mountains (Tizi n'Tichka Pass)
  • Guided tour of the ancient Ksar of Ait Ben Haddou
  • Visit to Ouarzazate and its famous film studios
  • Exploring the lush Dades Valley and towering Todra Gorges
  • Sunset camel trek into the Erg Chebbi dunes
  • Overnight in a luxury or traditional Berber desert camp
  • Stargazing in the clear desert sky

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Marrakech → High Atlas → Ait Ben Haddou → Dades Valley

Our journey begins with a drive through the High Atlas Mountains. We'll cross the Tizi n'Tichka pass (2,260m) with stops for panoramic views. Our first major stop is the world-famous Ait Ben Haddou, a fortified village that has served as a backdrop for many Hollywood blockbusters like Gladiator and Game of Thrones.

After lunch, we continue through Ouarzazate and the Valley of a Thousand Kasbahs to reach the Dades Valley, where we'll spend our first night.

Accommodation: Overnight in a charming Riad or Hotel in Dades Valley.

Day 2: Dades Valley → Todra Gorges → Merzouga (Sahara Desert)

After breakfast, we head to the Todra Gorges, a massive canyon with 300m high cliffs. You'll have time to walk along the river and enjoy the cool breeze. We then continue our drive towards Merzouga, passing through palm groves and traditional Berber villages.

In the late afternoon, we arrive at the edge of the dunes. Here, you'll mount your camel for a 1.5-hour trek into the heart of the Erg Chebbi dunes to witness a spectacular sunset. Dinner and music by the campfire await you at the camp.

Accommodation: Overnight in a Berber Desert Camp (Luxury or Standard options available).

Day 3: Merzouga → Draa Valley → Anti Atlas → Marrakech

Wake up early to catch the sunrise over the golden dunes—a truly magical moment. After breakfast at the camp, we trek back on camels (or 4x4) and begin our return journey. We'll travel through the Draa Valley, home to Morocco's longest river and millions of palm trees.

We cross the Anti-Atlas mountains and the Tizi n'Tnifift pass before returning to Marrakech in the late evening, concluding our desert adventure.

Accommodation & Meals

  • Night 1: Comfortable Hotel or Riad in Dades Valley (Private room with en-suite).
  • Night 2: Desert Camp in Erg Chebbi. Choose between a traditional nomad camp or a luxury camp with private showers.
  • Meals: Breakfast and Dinner are included daily. Lunches are taken at local restaurants along the way.

Fitness & Difficulty

This tour is categorized as Easy/Moderate. Most of the journey is by air-conditioned vehicle. The physical activity includes light walking in the Gorges and Ait Ben Haddou, and the camel trek (about 1.5 hours). It is suitable for all ages and fitness levels.

Important Information

  • Bring a small backpack for the overnight in the desert (your main luggage stays in the car).
  • Pack sun protection, a hat, and a scarf for the desert winds.
  • Warm clothing is essential for desert nights, even in summer.
  • Don't forget your camera for the stunning landscapes!
