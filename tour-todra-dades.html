Todra & Dades Gorges Expedition

4 Days Challenging Canyons & Valleys

Trip Overview

Discover the stunning geological formations of Morocco's own "Grand Canyon" on this 4-day expedition. You will hike through the dramatic vertical cliffs of the Todra Gorges and explore the winding green valleys of the Dades.

This tour offers a perfect mix of high ridge walking with spectacular panoramic views and valley floor trails through lush palm groves and ancient kasbahs. It is a journey through nature and history, revealing the life of Berber communities hidden within these massive rock formations.

Highlights

  • Walk beneath 300m vertical limestone cliffs
  • Traverse high ridges with canyon views
  • Explore lush green palm groves and gardens
  • Visit remote Berber villages and Kasbahs
  • See the famous "Monkey Fingers" rock formation

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Introductory Hike

Transfer to the Todra region. We begin with a short introductory hike through the palmery to warm up your legs and marvel at the towering orange cliffs.

Day 2: High Ridge Traverse

Today we hike up to the high ridges above the gorge. The path offers spectacular bird's-eye views of the canyon below. We encounter nomadic families grazing their flocks on the plateau.

Day 3: Into the Dades Valley

We descend into the Dades Valley, famous for its red rock formations. We hike through the unique "Monkey Fingers" area, a surreal landscape of eroded rock that looks like giant hands.

Day 4: Gardens & Kasbahs

Our final day is a gentle walk along the valley floor, winding through agricultural plots, gardens, and ancient crumbling Kasbahs, witnessing the daily life of the valley inhabitants before our tour concludes.

Accommodation & Meals

  • Accommodation: Comfortable local Guesthouses or Homestays with traditional hospitality.
  • Meals: Breakfast and Dinner included at accommodation. Picnic lunches provided during the hike.

What to Bring

  • Hiking boots with good ankle support
  • Sun hat and sunglasses
  • Daypack (20-30L)
  • Camera for the spectacular rock formations
  • Lightweight waterproof jacket

Fitness & Difficulty

Medium-High. The terrain is rocky and uneven. Some days involve steep ascents to the ridges. Good general fitness and sure-footedness are required.

Know Before You Go

  • The canyons can get hot in the afternoon; we start early.
  • Local culture is conservative; dress appropriately.

Best Time to Go

Spring (March-May) and Autumn (September-November) offer the most pleasant temperatures for hiking in the canyons.
