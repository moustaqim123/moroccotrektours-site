Agafay Desert: Day Trip to Overnight Stay

1 - 5 Days Options Easy / Family Friendly Agafay Desert

Trip Overview

Short on time but craving adventure? The Agafay Desert offers a taste of the wilderness just 45 minutes from Marrakech. Unlike the sandy Sahara, Agafay is a stone desert (reg) with dramatic rolling dunes that mimic the shape of sand dunes in rock and earth.

On this day trip, you will escape the city noise to breathe fresh air and enjoy the vast open horizons. You'll hike across the moon-like landscape, enjoy a traditional lunch in a tent, and have the option for a camel ride at sunset.

Highlights

  • Dramatic "moon-like" landscapes near Marrakech
  • Camel ride experience (optional)
  • Panoramic views of the snow-capped High Atlas
  • Traditional Tagine lunch in a desert tent
  • Tea ceremony at sunset

Experience Options in Agafay

Option 1: Agafay Day Trek & Lunch (Full Day)

  • 09:00: Pick-up from your hotel in Marrakech.
  • 10:00: Start of the guided trek through the "moon-like" stone dunes.
  • 12:30: Traditional Moroccan lunch in a luxury desert camp.
  • 14:30: Leisure time or optional camel ride/quad biking.
  • 17:00: Arrival back at your hotel in Marrakech.

Included: Transport, Professional Guide, Full Lunch, Mint Tea.

Option 2: Sunset Camel Ride & Magical Dinner

  • 16:00: Departure from Marrakech towards the desert.
  • 17:30: 1-hour camel trek during the golden hour/sunset.
  • 19:00: 3-course Moroccan dinner under the stars.
  • 20:30: Campfire and live Berber music performance.
  • 22:00: Transfer back to Marrakech.

Included: Transport, Camel Ride, Full Gourmet Dinner, Show.

Option 3: Luxury Desert Glamping (Overnight)

  • Day 1: Afternoon transfer to Agafay. Enjoy a camel trek or quad biking. Gourmet dinner and overnight in a luxury heated/cooled tent with private en-suite.
  • Day 2: Watch the sunrise over the Atlas Mountains. Enjoy a traditional breakfast in the desert before a relaxed transfer back to Marrakech.

Included: Full Board, Activities, Luxury Accommodation.

Option 4: Agafay & Atlas Foothills (3-5 Days)

  • Phase 1: Trek across the arid plains and stony dunes of Agafay.
  • Phase 2: Ascend into the Atlas foothills, passing through remote Berber villages like Tizi Mzik and Aroumd.
  • Phase 3: Final day in the lush Imlil Valley before returning to Marrakech.

Included: All meals, Guide, Mule support, Gîte/Camp stays.

Accommodation & Meals

  • Accommodation: Not applicable (Day Trip).
  • Meals: Traditional Lunch included. Vegetarian options available.

What to Bring

  • Sun hat and sunglasses (very open terrain)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sunscreen
  • Camera

Fitness & Difficulty

Easy. Suitable for all ages and fitness levels. The walking is gentle and on relatively flat terrain.

Know Before You Go

  • It can be very hot midday in summer; winter days are mild and pleasant.
  • This is a great option for families with children.

Best Time to Go

September to May. Summer (June-August) can be extremely hot, so early morning or sunset tours are preferred then.
